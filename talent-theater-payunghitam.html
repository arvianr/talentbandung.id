THE TALENT

Payung Hitam

The Black Umbrella Theater (TPH) was established in 1982, then inaugurated under the name of the Black Umbrella Foundation on November 29, 2007. TPH has produced more than 100 performances, and has attended various theater festivals both at home and abroad. Some of them :

Das Kampnagel-Sommer Hamburg-Germany festival (2003), Blackmoon, Amsterdam Netherlands (2007), Brisbane Powerhouse Thearee Festival, Australia (2007), International Festival and Seminar On Contemporary Performing Arts (ART SUMMIT INDONESIA VII) , RedEmptiness, Washington-Seatle-USA (2015), Migration Music Festival, Taipe-Taiwan (2015), Tainan Art Festival, Tainan-Taiwan (2015).
Some TPH works highlight national issues such as politics, economics, socio-culture and human rights, such as: Kaspar, Gendjer-Gendjer, Merah Bolong, DOM, Kata Kita Mati and others. In addition, TPH also highlights environmental and political issues, for example through his works such as "Air Tear Relief", "Blackmoon", "Noah Boat", "Soon," Margin "(2013); The latest is "POST HASTE" which has been and will be staged in Pekanbaru Riau (2016), Solo (2016), ISBI Bandung (2016), Cirebon (2016), Graha Bhakti Budaya Jakarta (17-18 May 2017), GK. Rumentang Siang Bandung (July 23, 2017), Europhalia, Belgium and the Netherlands (November-December 2017).
