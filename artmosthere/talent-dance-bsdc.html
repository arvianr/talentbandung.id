Bandung Street Dance Community

The history of B.S.D.C (Bandung Street Dance Community) started in early 2010, when two dancers: Gita Ahmad and Allifia Wardoyo met in cyberspace and found a similar interest in street dance. Based on the desire to practice in a street dance community, they met first for practice on February 14, 2010.

At this point BSDC is formed. With an initial member of 2 people, in a dance studio that turned out to be quite expensive to be paid for by just 2 people.

BSDC then gradually grows larger with the addition of the number of incoming members either through cyberspace info, or through word of mouth information. With the increasing number of members, BSDC also has more street dance flow families, such as Bboy, Locking, Popping, Tutting, Cwalk, Shuffler, and others.
