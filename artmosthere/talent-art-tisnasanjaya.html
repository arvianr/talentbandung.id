THE TALENT

Tisna Sanjaya

Tisna Sanjaya was born on January 28, 1958 in Bandung, West Java. Art education received by Tisna, among others, in the Graduate Program of Faculty of Art and Design, Bandung Institute of Technology (1979-1986), Diplom Kunst (1991-1994) and Meisterschueler (1997-1998) in Hohschuele Fur Bildende Kunste Braunschweig Germany, and Doctoral program at Institut Seni Indonesia, Yogyakarta (2008-2011). Since 1989, Tisna is also active as a lecturer at the Faculty of Art and Design, ITB.

Tisna first held a solo exhibition in 1982, entitled "Everyday life" on the East Cikapundung road, Bandung. In 1985, Tisna's work was first exhibited in a joint exhibition in the "Asean Youth Painting Workshop and Exhibition" in Yogyakarta. Since then Tisna's work began to be frequently exhibited in various exhibitions, both inside and outside the country.

Tisna has received numerous awards, including: "Best 10, Indonesian Art Awards 1996"; "Sponsor award at the Sapporo International Print Competition 1997" in Japan; "Best Painter Phillip Morris Indonesia Art Awards 1997"; "Best Lecturer" at Bandung Institute of Technology (1997); and "Artist Award" by West Java Provincial Government (2006).

In 2012, Tisna's work is on display in a joint exhibition entitled "Mother Earth an Earth-Day-Inspired Exhibition" at Green Artspace, Jakarta. Rifky Effendy, in his curatorial note, mentions one of Tisna's works that touches environmental issues is his project called "Cigondewah Cultural Center". In the project, Tisna built a simple building on a plot of land between factory buildings in the south of Bandung. There Tisna interacts with the surrounding community, inviting them to dialogue and invite art. Rifky said that Tisna's activities can provide environmental awareness to the local community, because it is done organically and with simple communication.
